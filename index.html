Frequently Asked Questions

What are your classmates asking about with this reunion

1

How should we dress?

It's the summer time so dress comfortably.

Business casual is always a safe choice. It's not a black tie affair!

2

How should we pay?

We are in the process of trying to setup payment online via a credit card. However, if you prefer to pay by check please make checks payable to:

3

How can I get involved?

If you'd like to get involved with any phase please contact Mark Ceraolo, Jackie Dight, Andre Barnabei, or Danielle Waltz.

We are always looking for volunteers!

4

Who made the decision on where to have the reunion?

Using a Facebook poll and contacting multiple venues, it was determined that having it local to Weirton was the best option and Undo's had the available date.
