Frequently Asked Questions

What are your classmates asking about with this reunion

1

How should we dress?

It's the summer time so dress comfortably.

Business casual is always a safe choice. It's not a black tie affair!

2

How should we pay?

Starting now, you can click this link: Register where you'll register for this event. Within that site you'll be able to choose to pay via credit card or check. The mailing address will be listed there.

3

Can I show up at the door without a reservation?

We highly recommend making your reservation prior to the night of the reunion due to seating and cost. However, if you can't register beforehand, we'll accept cash and check on the night of the reunion.

4

Will there be a Reunion class picture?

Yes of course! Newbrough Photo will be there to take the class picture. We should have copies ready by the end of the night. Cost is $10 and you'll be able to pay in advance when you register.

5

Will I receive a payment confirmation?

If you pay by credit card, you will receive an email confirmation from Third Party site.

6

Will I receive tickets in the mail?

No. We will have a check-in at the door under the last name you registered with.
